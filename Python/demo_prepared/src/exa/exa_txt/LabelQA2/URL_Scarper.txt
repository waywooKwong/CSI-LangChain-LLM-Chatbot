The multicore VCO\n\nuses an internal autocalibration routine that allows the PLL to\n\nselect the necessary settings and lock in approximately 400 μs.\n\nThe reference input to the PLL employs a differentially excited\n\n50 MHz crystal oscillator on the evaluation board. Alternatively,\n\nthe reference input can be driven by an external single-ended\n\nreference source. The phase frequency detector (PFD) comparison\n\nfrequency of the PLL operates up to 50 MHz.\n\nThe ADMV4420 is fabricated on a silicon germanium (SiGe),\n\nbipolar complementary metal-oxide semiconductor (BiCMOS)\n\nprocess and is available in a 32-lead, RoHS compliant, 5 mm ×\n\n5 mm LFCSP with an exposed pad. The device is specified over\n\nthe −40°C to +85°C temperature range on a 5 V power supply.\n\nADMV4420-EVALZ User Guide\n\nUG-1404\n\nRev. 0 | Page 2 of 20\n\nTABLE OF CONTENTS\n\nFeatures
